# Marketing Analytics Executive Dashboard
### Multi-Channel Paid Media Performance Analysis

## Overview
This project recreates a professional marketing analytics dashboard 
inspired by Improvado's Executive Summary view. It provides a 
centralized view of paid media performance across multiple channels 
and data sources for Q1 2023.

**Live Dashboard:** [View on Looker Studio](https://datastudio.google.com/reporting/6dc31d8a-a48f-4db7-81da-bce22597d2a4)

---

## Tools Used
- **Google Sheets** — Data generation and structuring
- **Looker Studio** — Dashboard and visualizations

---

## Dashboard Components

### KPI Cards
8 top-level metrics with period-over-period comparison:
Spend, CPM, CTR, CPC, Video Views, Impressions, 
Conversions, and Conversion Rate.

### Channel Performance Table
Performance breakdown by channel: Programmatic, 
Paid Search, Paid Social, and Organic.

### Data Source Performance Table
Impressions and CTR by platform: Amazon Ad Server, 
StackAdapt, LinkedIn Ads, Facebook, Google Display, 
Bing Ads, and Google Search Ads.

### Campaign Performance Table
Individual campaign metrics including Impressions and CTR.

### Monthly Trends Chart
Time series visualization of impressions by channel 
across January–December 2023.

---

## Key Insights

**Efficiency despite higher costs:** CPM increased, 
but CPC decreased and conversions improved — indicating 
better targeting and creative performance.

**Channel shift:** Paid Search grew 30.7% while 
Paid Social dropped 25.6% — suggesting a budget 
reallocation opportunity toward search.

**Emerging source:** Amazon Ad Server showed 201% 
growth — a channel worth monitoring and potentially 
scaling.

**Seasonality:** Impressions peaked in July and 
gradually declined toward year-end, with Paid Search 
remaining the most stable channel throughout.

---

## Data Generation Approach
Dummy data was generated to reflect realistic paid 
media benchmarks across 4 channels, 7 data sources, 
and 8 campaigns. All metrics were structured in 
Google Sheets and connected directly to Looker Studio.
